# Personas

## Submitter

Convention production staff capturing lessons while memory is fresh — often between other job responsibilities and without deep convention-production experience.

**Goals:** Low-friction capture; enough contact information for follow-up; visibility into what others have already reported.

## Team lead

Leads who organize feedback after submission — classifying, enriching, and curating the archive for future readers.

**Goals:** Post-submit classification without blocking initial capture; tools to tag, cross-link, and organize the corpus.

## Future reader

Planning leads or staff preparing for a future convention cycle, possibly years later, with limited institutional memory.

**Goals:** Discover what worked, what did not, and hard-won suggestions across technical, logistical, attendee, vendor, morale, and safety domains.

## Maintainer

DAIR or technical staff who examine cloud-backed data from this repo and evolve submission UI, lookups, and taxonomy.

**Goals:** Inspect submissions and taxonomy from repo tooling; ship UI and lookup updates; generate HTML time-capsule exports.

## Number One (agent)

Primary coding agent working in this repository (Cursor, Copilot, Claude Code, etc.). Follows [AGENTS.md](../AGENTS.md), reads canonical skills before acting, and executes the active plan when told **make it so**.
